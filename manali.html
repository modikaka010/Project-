The Allure Grand

Allure Grand Resort is a Luxury 4 star riverside resort nestled on Manali Rohtang higway at the bank of Beas River & facing the Rohtang snow glacier. The approximate height is about 7,100 feet. This riverside resort in Manali is inspired by the soul of the river front life and lush green valleys filled with forests that add to the serenity of the location. The resort spreads over 1.5 acres, having lush green gardens overlooking river beas and the premises is loaded with apple and apricots vegetation which make the lawns a perfect place to enjoy the tranquillity of this beautiful hill station.

And your journey to this world begins as you enter through the Gate of Confluence, inspired from the great inscriptions of Mauryan period with Gold foils and a palette of bright colours inspired from Buddhist monasteries located in different areas of Himachal Pradesh.
